methodology

the clawhub audit pipeline runs nightly and consists of three phases:

  1. differential download — fetch the current skill manifest, identify new/updated skills, download their contents
  2. regex scan — match against 40+ injection and exfiltration patterns derived from snyk, cisco, and kaspersky research
  3. llm triage — classify each regex finding as malicious, benign, or suspicious using claude haiku with full skill context

this approach reduces the ~72% false positive rate of regex-only scanning to actionable results for human review.
