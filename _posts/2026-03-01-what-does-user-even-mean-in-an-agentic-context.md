What does 'user' even mean in an agentic context?

Most teams can't answer that. Which means their AI has no real access control.

Agentic Control Plane gives you per-user access controls out of the box — for your employees and their AI agents. When your team requests data via AI, it only returns what they (or their agent) are allowed to see. 

If your team asks for the sales pipeline…

— Execs get the entire company pipeline
— Sales Reps get their individual pipeline
— Finance Analysts get no data back — they don’t have CRM access
— Ops Engineers get no data back — they don’t have CRM access

Access control is one of six layers of Agentic Control Plane governance.
