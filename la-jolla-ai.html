la jolla ai build

a hands-on group for people who build with AI

when & where

every other tuesday, 4–6pm
Queenstown Bistro
4545 La Jolla Village Drive, San Diego

look for the group with laptops on the patio. i'll be in a purple shirt.

what this is

a small, hands-on group. no talks, no pitches, no panels.

developers, builders, and founders show up to build together — on code, products, or systems. we often discuss new ideas and do early-stage exploratory work to pressure-test them.

non-technical folk with solid AI product ideas are also welcome.

what to bring

  • your laptop
  • something you're working on, or an idea you want to explore
  • questions, problems, half-baked prototypes — all fair game
